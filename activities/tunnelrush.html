Tunnel Rush

Description

Tunnel rush is a colorful and fast paced game that keeps the player on edge the whole time they are playing.

Gameplay

In Tunnel Rush, the player speeds down a tunnel avoiding obstacles at a high pace.

Difficulty

The player progressively speeds up, making surviving more challenging the longer they play.

Design and Controls

Tunnel Rush can be played with wasd or arrow keys.
