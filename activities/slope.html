Slope

Description

Slope is an online game that challenges players with a fast-paced, endless downhill journey.

Gameplay

In this game, you control a ball as it rolls down an infinite slope, dodging obstacles and navigating through a futuristic green-neon landscape. The objective is simple: keep the ball from falling off the edge or crashing into obstacles for as long as possible to acheive the highest score.

Difficulty

The slope gets steeper and the speed increases as you progress, making the game increasingly challenging.

Design and Controls

With simple controls, nearly anyone can pick it up, simply with the use of the arrow keys, or a, q, or d
