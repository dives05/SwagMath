1v1.lol

Description

1v1.LOL is a free-to-play, online, third-person shooter, multiplayer, Battle Royale game that combines shooting, building, and strategy:

Gameplay

Players compete in fast-paced 1v1 matches against random players or friends, using weapons and construction modes to survive and outsmart their opponents. Construction modes include ramps, walls, and ceilings that can be used for cover. Players can also destroy anything on the map.

Customization

Players can customize their characters with different skins, emotes, and sprays. They can also choose from a variety of champions, each with unique abilities.

Difficulty

The game adapts difficulty levels by matching players up against opponents of similar skill levels so that players of all kind can enjoy it.

Versions

1v1.LOL has versions for different ages, and the game will direct players to the appropriate version based on their location.

Availability

1v1.LOL is available on iOS, PC, and Steam. It was initially released in May 2020 by JustPlay.LOL LTD.
